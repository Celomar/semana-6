Day 612: Relic

It's been well over 600 days since Avin and I took up the life of bounty hunters, travelling though to the furthest reaches of the galaxy in the pursuit of loot, fame, and -more importantly- Adventure.

This life got pretty boring past the 300th day and we've just been doing the rounds ever since. It's become a job now, not a hobby. It's no longer fun. But now, 612th day we've come across something worth our time:

This is called a "video-game" it is from Earthling origin. These are some of the most fun artifacts we've come across, so much so I don't think I'll be collecting the bounty for it. We'll keep it. This one in particular goes by the name "MEGAMAN X" and it's about a fighting robot that needs to defeat some...other...robots? It's really fun, it is not about the story with these, I wish we could explain.

-Zadrak

Day 615: Hunting something else now

Forget bounty hunting, we're collecting these now. This one is called "Earthbound" and it plays very differently, a "turn-based" game if you will. It also very well emulates life on earth which I find particularly endearing.

If we're putting all of our focus into hunting these down for our own pleasure...There must be a way to monetize this. Maybe if we were to broadcast these, as if it was a show in which we get to play for a watchful audience..That would be interesting. But would we even get an audience? Would anyone watch a couple of guys playing videogames online? We'll never now unless we try.

-Zadrak
